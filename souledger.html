FAQ

Questions worth
asking.

How is SoulLedger different from journaling apps like Day One?

Journaling apps store your words. SoulLedger structures your life. We combine reflection with analytics — so you don't just record your days, you understand them across weeks, months, and years.

How long does it take to use daily?

The daily entry is designed to take under 5 minutes. The weekly review, around 20. The depth comes from consistency over time, not from hours spent in the app each day.

Is my data private? Who can see it?

No one but you. SoulLedger is built with a privacy-first architecture. Your reflections are yours. We don't sell, share, or analyze your personal content. Ever.

How is this different from Tony Robbins or mindset content?

Motivational content gives you energy. SoulLedger gives you data. We're not in the business of inspiration — we're in the business of self-knowledge. What you measure, you can improve.

When does SoulLedger launch?

We're in active development right now. Waitlist members will be the first to access the beta and will help shape the final product. No launch date locked in yet — quality over speed.

What if I miss days or weeks of tracking?

Life happens. SoulLedger is designed for real humans, not perfect robots. You can back-fill entries, and the system works even with gaps — consistency over perfection is the philosophy.
